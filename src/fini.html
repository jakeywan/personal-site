Fini

Fini is making new myths for the digital age. We started by releasing a sold out 10k NFT collection with a massive amount of detail and variety. These NFTs are wired up dynamically to the live prices of 10 different crypto coins. I built a binary search algorithm on Chainlink's price oracles to perform the bulk of this functionality on chain (though we use an off-chain version with Coingecko APIs for better speed). We've also produced around 100 different animated states, resulting in about 1 million animations, all of which are stored publicly on IPFS nodes. Browse the collection on our custom marketplace (React, Next.js, 0xReservoir, Coingecko API). Check out the smart contracts: 0x5a0121a0a21232ec0d024dab9017314509026480 and 0x99eb96dee9Ad0930994f1f12e2c82f6E80985356.
We released a mobile app for iOS (native Swift code) that lets you track your favorite coins, stocks, ETFs, and even NFT floor prices. Users can watch their Finis emote based on asset performance, and can put Finis on their Apple watch, iPhone home screen widgets, and even desktop.
We've also created some new Finis, in partnership with good friends. We partnered with Rainbow to create Bow, a new Fini that unlocked a new gas tracking feature in our app.
