Fast Food Nouns

An experimental expansion of the Nouns universe, exploring the concept of on chain customizations for NFTs. Owners could mint, trade, and purchase exclusive NFT clothes and accessories and "wear" them on the blockchain.
Owners could purchase wearables and "dress" their Noun in them. The smart contract would read the worn clothes, unwrap the SVGs and bundle them together and return a single SVG representation of the Noun.
Users could design and submit new accessories or clothes to list on the open market.
I ultimately wrote a staking contract which let users deposit their Fast Food Noun and bridge it to Polygon, where users could buy/sell/trade new clothes with far lower gas costs.
We partnered with some high profile DAOs and artists to produce custom clothing for members.
